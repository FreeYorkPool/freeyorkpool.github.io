How To Stake Your Cardano ADA

There are many ways to delegate/stake your ADA, you can choose either Daedalus or Yoroi as your private wallet.

Daedalus

  1. Download the Daedalus Wallet.
  2. First time launching the Daedalus wallet, the wallet will sync with the blockchain.
  3. After synchronization is complete, follow instruction to create / restore a wallet.
  4. Make sure the wallet has been funded by your ADA, or you can fund your wallet by creating an address in the "Receive" tab.
  5. Click the "Stake Pools" tab in the left panel.
  6. Search "YORK" and click "Delegate to this pool" button.

Yoroi

  1. Download the Yoroi Light Wallet.
  2. Find and click the "download" button then choose your preferred browser. (Yoroi wallet will be installed as extension/add-on on your browser)
  3. Launch the Yoroi wallet extension within the browser.
  4. Follow instruction to connect / create / restore your Cardano wallet.
  5. Make sure the wallet has been funded by your ADA, or you can fund your wallet by creating an address in the "Receive" tab.
  6. Click the "Delegation List" tab and search "YORK" in the Search By Id or Name form.
  7. The Result will be that pool and click the "Delegate" button.

Yoroi Mobile

  1. Search and download the Yoroi mobile wallet app from your device: iOS | Android
  2. Launch the Yoroi app and follow instruction to connect / create / restore your Cardano wallet.
  3. Make sure the wallet has funded by your ADA, or you can fund your wallet by creating an address in the "Receive" tab.
  4. Go to the "Dashboard" tab and choose "Go to Staking Center".
  5. Search "YORK" and then choose the "Delegate" button.
